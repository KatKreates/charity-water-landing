Meet Umu — Thanks to a solar-powered water system funded by charity: water, Umu Bouare no longer spends hours collecting unsafe water—instead, she runs her own hair business and teaches hygiene in her village. Her story shows how one donation can create lasting impact through clean water, economic empowerment, and community health.

Meet Natalia — At just 15, Natalia became the President of her village’s water committee in Mozambique — a role made possible by access to clean water funded by charity: water donors. Once burdened by daily water collection, she now attends school full-time and leads her community with pride and purpose.
